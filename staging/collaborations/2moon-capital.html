QUERY_SECURITY_FAQ
[01]What are signs a development company doesn't understand trading systems?
Red flags include vague answers about market data, promising fixed timelines for strategy performance, not mentioning risk controls, misusing backtesting vs live trading terminology, and lack of experience with broker APIs like IBKR or FIX.
[02]What testing environment costs should I budget for paper trading?
While paper trading accounts are often free, the cloud infrastructure (AWS) and real-time market data feeds (e.g., Bloomberg, Refinitiv, or IBKR's paid tiers) can cost $200–$1,000+ monthly depending on data volume and latency needs.
[03]How many iterations should I expect before a production-ready trading system?
Usually three to eight iterations: initial prototype, paper trading, real-time simulation, limited-capital live testing, and then full deployment with monitoring.
[04]What happens to my trading algorithms IP – who owns the code?
In our model, the client owns 100% of the IP from day one. This is critical in trading because the business value lies in the proprietary logic of the algorithms, not just the execution platform.
[05]How much does it cost to build an algorithmic trading platform?
A custom algorithmic trading platform typically ranges from $80K to $450K depending on complexity, order types, latency requirements, data integrations, and security needs.
