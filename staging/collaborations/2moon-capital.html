QUERY_SECURITY_FAQ
[01]What are the signs that a development company doesn't understand trading systems?
Red flags include vague answers about market data, promises of fixed timelines for strategy performance, failure to mention risk controls, misuse of backtesting vs. live trading terminology, and lack of experience with broker APIs like IBKR or FIX.
[02]What testing environment costs should I budget for simulated trading?
While simulated trading accounts are often free, cloud infrastructure (AWS) and real-time market data sources can cost between $200 and $1,000 USD monthly depending on data volume.
[03]How many iterations should I expect before having a production-ready trading system?
Usually three to eight iterations: initial prototype, paper trading, real-time simulation, limited capital testing, and then full deployment with monitoring.
[04]What happens to the intellectual property of my trading algorithms?
In our model, the client owns 100% of the intellectual property from day one. The business value resides in the proprietary logic of the algorithms, not just the execution platform.
[05]How much does it cost to build an algorithmic trading platform?
A custom trading platform typically ranges between $80k and $450k USD depending on complexity, order types, latency requirements, data integrations, and security needs.
