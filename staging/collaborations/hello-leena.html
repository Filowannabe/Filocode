[01]Why build a custom EMR instead of using a standard health SaaS?
Mental health clinics often have unique flows for matching therapists with patients based on their identity (BIPOC). A custom EMR allows for specific matching algorithms, proprietary reports, and total control over the client experience.
[02]How do you handle security for telemedicine video sessions?
We use HIPAA-compliant video SDKs with end-to-end encryption, ensuring that therapy sessions are never recorded or stored on intermediate servers without explicit authorization.
[03]Should I hire a full team or start with a single developer for MVP validation?
For an EMR, you need a multidisciplinary team (Frontend, Backend, Security, Design) from day one. Starting with a single developer often leads to architectural shortcuts that turn into massive technical debt when handling real patient data.
